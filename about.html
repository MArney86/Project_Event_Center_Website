Welcome to the Community Concert Center

Where everlasting memories are made. Your local concert and live-event venue with a focus on the community and high-quality experiences.

Our History & Mission

We here at the Community Concert Center pride ourselves on bringing high-quality entertainment to the local community.

Originally opened in 1995, and purchased by the local government in 2008, we pride ourselves on attention to detail and quality customer service to keep our promise of giving wonderful experiences and memories to the community of neighbors we serve and the multitudes of guests who visit us.

Our mission is to provide a premier entertainment venue that enriches the cultural life of our community while maintaining the highest standards of customer service and venue excellence.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of professionals works tirelessly to ensure every event is a memorable experience. From our event coordinators to our technical crew, customer service staff, and venue management, each member is committed to excellence in serving our community.
