About Us:

We here at the Community Concert Center pride ourselves on bringing high-quality entertinment to the local community.

Originally opened in 1995, and purchased by the local government in 2008, we pride ourselves on attention to detail and quality customer service to keep our promise of giving wonder experiences and memories to the community of neighbors we serve and the multitudes of guests who visit us.
